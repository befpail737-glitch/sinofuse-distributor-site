About LiTong

Your Trusted Partner in Circuit Protection Solutions Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, LiTong began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between world-class circuit protection manufacturers and businesses that need reliable, high-quality components. What started as a small trading company in Vietnam has grown into a global distribution network spanning 13 countries across Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

In 2015, we established our strategic partnership with Sinofuse (Xi'an Sinofuse Electric Co., Ltd.), one of China's leading manufacturers of fuses and circuit protection devices. This partnership elevated LiTong to Tier-1 distributor status, giving us direct access to Sinofuse's complete product portfolio and technical resources.

Today, LiTong serves over 500 corporate clients worldwide, from automotive OEMs and renewable energy developers to industrial automation companies and telecommunications providers. Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional technical support has made us the preferred Sinofuse distributor for businesses seeking reliable circuit protection solutions.
