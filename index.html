Complete Sinofuse Product Portfolio

As an authorized Sinofuse distributor, LiTong provides comprehensive access to the complete range of Sinofuse circuit protection solutions. Sinofuse (Xi'an Sinofuse Electric Co., Ltd.) is a leading Chinese manufacturer specializing in high-quality fuses for electric vehicles, solar power systems, industrial applications, and consumer electronics.

Our product portfolio includes EV fuses designed for electric vehicle battery protection, PV fuses for solar inverter systems, industrial fuses for heavy machinery, and electronic fuses for circuit board protection. Each product meets international standards including UL, CE, CCC, and TUV certifications.

Electric Vehicle Fuse Solutions

The rapid growth of the electric vehicle market demands reliable circuit protection. Our EV fuse series includes high-speed fuses rated from 10A to 1000A, capable of protecting battery management systems, charging infrastructure, and power distribution units. These fuses feature low arc voltage, high breaking capacity, and excellent current limiting characteristics essential for EV safety.

Solar & Photovoltaic Protection

Solar energy systems require specialized protection against overcurrent conditions. Our PV fuse selection covers string fuses, combiner box fuses, and inverter protection devices rated for DC voltages up to 1500V. These fuses are engineered to withstand harsh outdoor conditions while maintaining reliable protection for solar installations worldwide.
