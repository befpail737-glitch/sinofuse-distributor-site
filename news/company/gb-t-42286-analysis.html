5G Telecom Power Distribution Unit (PDU) Precise Protection Solution

The rollout of 5G infrastructure demands higher power density and absolute reliability from power distribution units. A fault in one branch cannot be allowed to cascade and cause a system-wide outage. This solution employs high-performance, fast-acting Sinofuse fuses that provide precise circuit isolation, ensuring maximum uptime for critical telecommunications equipment while optimizing space in dense PDU designs.

Solution Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • Precise Tripping Characteristics: Extremely tight manufacturing tolerances ensure selective coordination, meaning only the fuse closest to the fault opens.
  • High Power Density: Compact fuse footprints allow for more protection channels in a smaller PDU chassis.
  • Low Power Loss: Minimizes heat generation within the PDU, improving system reliability and reducing cooling requirements.
  • High DC Interrupting Capacity: Safely clears faults in standard -48VDC telecom power systems.
