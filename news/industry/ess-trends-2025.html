Key Technology Trends Shaping 2025

Several technological developments are converging to transform the ESS landscape:

1. Battery Chemistry Evolution

Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) Dominance

LFP technology is capturing increasing market share due to improved safety characteristics, longer cycle life, and reduced material costs. The shift toward LFP is driving changes in system architecture and protection requirements:

  • Lower nominal voltage per cell requiring different protection strategies
  • Improved thermal stability reducing fire suppression requirements
  • Longer cycle life extending system economic viability
  • Reduced cobalt dependency improving supply chain stability
