Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the EV315-M-4Q suitable for electric vehicle applications?

The EV315-M-4Q is specifically designed for EV applications with features like AEC-Q200 automotive qualification, high vibration resistance, and fast response time. It's optimized for the unique electrical characteristics and environmental conditions found in electric vehicles.

What is the operating temperature range for this fuse?

The EV315-M-4Q operates effectively in temperatures from -40°C to +85°C, making it suitable for use in various climates and conditions, including extreme cold and hot environments.

Can this fuse be used in energy storage systems?

Yes, the EV315-M-4Q is also suitable for energy storage systems that operate at 450V DC or below. Its high breaking capacity and reliable performance make it an excellent choice for protecting battery energy storage systems.
