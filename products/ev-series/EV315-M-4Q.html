Product Description

The Sinofuse EV315-M-4Q is a high-performance fuse specifically engineered for the rigorous demands of modern electric vehicle main circuits. It provides reliable overcurrent protection for battery packs and PDUs, ensuring system safety even under extreme short-circuit conditions. Its robust mechanical design ensures stability in high-vibration environments.
