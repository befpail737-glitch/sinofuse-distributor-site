The Sinofuse BS88-4 series provides reliable and cost-effective general-purpose protection for industrial and commercial electrical distribution systems. Product Description

The Sinofuse RT16-00 fuse link is the smallest in the NH fuse family, offering high breaking capacity (up to 120kA) in a compact package. These gG class fuses are the standard choice for industrial low-voltage distribution and machinery protection. They are designed to be used with a corresponding NH00 fuse base, which allows for easy DIN rail mounting and safe, tool-free replacement of the fuse link. Dual indicator pins provide clear visual identification of a blown fuse.
