New Energy Vehicle Fuses (EV Fuses)

As an official Sinofuse distributor, we provide a complete range of New Energy Vehicle fuses. This series is engineered specifically for the harsh operating conditions of Electric Vehicles (EV), Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV), and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEV), covering critical applications from the main battery pack and Power Distribution Unit (PDU) to the motor controller and on-board charger (OBC). Sinofuse EV series fuses are trusted by leading automotive manufacturers for their exceptional DC breaking capacity, superior resistance to vibration and mechanical shock, and high reliability. Whether you need protection for the main circuit or auxiliary circuits, or are looking for fuses that meet the latest automotive standards like ISO 8820, our team can provide expert Sinofuse EV fuse selection support.
