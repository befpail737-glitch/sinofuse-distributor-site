Photovoltaic Inverter DC Side Protection Solution

As solar installations push towards higher voltages like 1500VDC to maximize efficiency, robust and specialized DC side protection becomes paramount. This solution leverages Sinofuse's gPV-rated fuses to provide reliable protection against reverse currents and low-overcurrent faults common in PV arrays, safeguarding expensive inverters and ensuring compliance with IEC and UL photovoltaic standards.

Solution Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • Certified gPV Protection: Designed to safely interrupt low-level fault currents typical of PV strings, a condition where standard fuses may fail.
  • High Voltage DC Capability: Specifically engineered and tested for systems up to 1500VDC, ensuring safe operation at modern voltage levels.
  • Low Power Loss: Optimized design minimizes watt loss, improving the overall energy efficiency and yield of the solar installation.
  • Global Standards Compliance: Meets key international standards for photovoltaic protection (IEC 60269-6, UL 248), simplifying global product certification.
