New Energy Vehicle Battery Pack (BMS) Protection Solution

Protecting the high-voltage battery pack is the most critical safety function in an electric vehicle. This solution provides a robust framework for overcurrent protection against both catastrophic short circuits and sustained overloads, utilizing the automotive-grade Sinofuse EV series to ensure system integrity and compliance with standards like ISO 26262.
