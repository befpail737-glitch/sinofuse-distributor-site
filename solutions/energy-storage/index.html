BESS Protection

Solution Overview

Large-scale Battery Energy Storage Systems present significant circuit protection challenges due to high DC voltages and massive short-circuit current potential. This comprehensive solution provides coordinated protection for battery racks and DC combiners using Sinofuse fuses with extreme breaking capacity.
