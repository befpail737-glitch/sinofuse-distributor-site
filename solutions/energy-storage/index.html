Containerized ESS DC Side Protection Solution

Large-scale Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) present significant circuit protection challenges due to high DC voltages and massive short-circuit current potential. This solution provides a coordinated protection strategy for battery racks and DC combiners, using Sinofuse fuses with high DC breaking capacity and excellent cycling performance to ensure safety and maximize system availability.
