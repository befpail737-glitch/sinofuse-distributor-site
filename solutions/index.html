A comprehensive solution for protecting high-voltage electric vehicle battery packs. This design uses automotive-grade Sinofuse EV fuses to safeguard against overcurrent and short-circuit events, ensuring passenger safety and system longevity in demanding vibration and temperature environments. Containerized ESS DC Side Protection Solution

This solution addresses the unique challenges of large-scale Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS). It provides a coordinated protection scheme for battery racks, DC combiners, and PCS inverters using high-performance Sinofuse fuses. The design ensures high breaking capacity for DC faults and long-term reliability, maximizing system uptime and protecting high-value assets.

Photovoltaic Inverter Protection Solution

A specialized solution for protecting the DC side of central and string solar inverters. Featuring Sinofuse PV series fuses with gPV characteristics, this design effectively isolates faulty strings, prevents reverse current damage, and ensures compliance with global photovoltaic safety standards. It is suitable for systems up to 1500VDC.
