Rail Traffic Auxiliary Power System (APS) High-Reliability Protection Solution

Auxiliary Power Systems (APS) in modern rolling stock power everything from HVAC and lighting to communication and control systems. These systems operate under constant, severe mechanical shock and vibration. This solution utilizes specially designed, high-reliability Sinofuse fuses that ensure uninterrupted, safe operation even in the harshest rail environments, meeting stringent industry standards for durability and performance.

Solution Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • Extreme Vibration Resistance: Built with robust internal construction to withstand the constant shock and vibration specified in railway standards like IEC 61373.
  • High Breaking Capacity: Capable of safely handling the high fault currents present in powerful onboard electrical systems.
  • Thermal Shock Durability: Withstands rapid and wide temperature fluctuations experienced in diverse operating climates without degradation.
  • Long-Term Reliability: Low power loss and stable performance characteristics ensure a long service life with minimal maintenance.

The selection of fuses for rail applications demands a focus on mechanical and electrical robustness. The following series are designed for these conditions.
