4. Installation and Maintenance Best Practices

‚úÖ Best Practices

  • Use only gPV-rated fuses for PV applications
  • Ensure proper current derating for ambient temperature
  • Implement coordinated protection strategies
  • Plan for thermal management in combiner boxes
  • Document protection coordination studies
  • Schedule regular maintenance inspections

‚ùå Common Mistakes

  • Using general-purpose fuses in PV applications
  • Oversizing fuses for "extra safety margin"
  • Ignoring temperature derating requirements
  • Poor coordination between protection levels
  • Inadequate consideration of reverse current
  • Neglecting long-term performance monitoring
